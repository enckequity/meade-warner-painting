# Meade Warner Painting — Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

**Company Name:** Meade Warner Painting
**Tagline:** "A Fresh Coat Done Right"
**Location:** 924 11th Ave, Huntington, WV 25701
**Phone:** (304) 955-8802
**Voice:** Clean, confident, approachable. Speaks like a craftsman who takes pride in their work — not salesy, just honest about doing great work. Every word should feel as deliberate as a clean brush stroke.

---

## Color Palette

### Primary — Slate Navy
A deep, confident navy that communicates precision and professionalism.
- **Slate Navy:** `#1B2D45` (hsl 213 42% 19%)
- Used for: headers, nav background, primary text, hero overlays

### Secondary — Warm Sage
A muted, earthy green that grounds the brand in the natural WV landscape.
- **Warm Sage:** `#4A6741` (hsl 134 21% 34%)
- Used for: accents, section backgrounds, badges

### Accent — Amber Gold
A warm, high-contrast accent that pops against the navy and sage.
- **Amber Gold:** `#D4923A` (hsl 35 63% 53%)
- Used for: CTAs, highlights, hover states, important links

### Neutrals
- **Canvas White:** `#FAFAF8` — page backgrounds, card backgrounds
- **Warm Gray 100:** `#F0EFEB` — section alternating backgrounds
- **Warm Gray 300:** `#C8C5BD` — borders, dividers
- **Warm Gray 500:** `#8A8680` — secondary text
- **Warm Gray 700:** `#4A4844` — body text
- **Warm Gray 900:** `#1A1918` — primary text, headings

### Forbidden Colors
- No purple-to-blue gradients
- No neon anything
- No generic startup blue (#007bff)
- No pure black (#000000) — use Warm Gray 900 instead
- No pure white (#ffffff) — use Canvas White instead

---

## Typography

### Headings — DM Serif Display
A refined, modern serif with character and warmth. Communicates craftsmanship and attention to detail. Available on Google Fonts.
- Weights: 400 (regular)
- Usage: H1, H2, H3, hero headlines, section titles

### Body — Plus Jakarta Sans
A geometric sans-serif with excellent readability and a modern, approachable feel. Pairs beautifully with DM Serif Display.
- Weights: 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold)
- Usage: body text, nav links, buttons, captions, metadata

### Type Scale
- H1: 3.5rem / 56px (hero) — DM Serif Display
- H2: 2.5rem / 40px (section titles) — DM Serif Display
- H3: 1.5rem / 24px (card titles) — DM Serif Display
- Body: 1.125rem / 18px — Plus Jakarta Sans
- Small: 0.875rem / 14px — Plus Jakarta Sans
- Caption: 0.75rem / 12px — Plus Jakarta Sans

## Logo

The logo is a clean, typographic wordmark with a subtle paint brush accent mark. The brush stroke sits above the "M" in "Meade" as a horizontal accent — suggesting a fresh stroke of paint.

- Format: SVG
- Colors: Slate Navy on light backgrounds, Canvas White on dark backgrounds
- Minimum size: 120px wide
- Clear space: equal to the height of the "M" on all sides

---

## Photography Direction

- Real job-site photography preferred over stock
- Natural lighting, no heavy filters
- Show: freshly painted rooms, crisp trim work, exterior transformations, paint tools, color swatches
- Feel: the satisfaction of a clean, finished job
- Avoid: overly staged magazine shots, models posing, generic stock imagery

---

## Design Principles

1. **Precision** — Clean lines, consistent spacing, deliberate alignment. The site should demonstrate the same attention to detail as a perfect paint job.
2. **Warmth** — Not cold or corporate. This is a local business run by a real person.
3. **Trust** — Every element should build confidence. If the website looks sloppy, why would you trust them to paint your house?
4. **Clarity** — Clear hierarchy, obvious CTAs, no confusion about what to do next.
5. **Local** — This is Huntington, WV. The site should feel grounded in the community, not generic.

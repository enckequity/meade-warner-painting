## Strategy Overview

The Huntington, WV painting market has extremely low SEO competition. Most local painters have no website or rely on Wix/Facebook pages with thin content. **The play:** Own the local search results with deep, authentic, location-targeted content that no competitor is producing.

## Content Strategy Recommendations

### Phase 1: Foundation (Launch)

Build these pages before launch. Each should be 800-1200 words with local keywords naturally integrated.

1. **Homepage** -- Target "painter Huntington WV" and "painting company Huntington WV"
2. **Interior Painting** service page
3. **Exterior Painting** service page
4. **Cabinet Painting** service page
5. **Pressure Washing** service page
6. **Drywall Repair** service page
7. **Commercial Painting** service page
8. **About / Our Story** page -- owner bio, team, insurance, licenses
9. **Service Areas** page with all Tri-State locations listed
10. **Contact / Free Estimate** page with schema markup
11. **Gallery** page with before/after photos organized by project type

### Phase 2: City Landing Pages (Month 1-2)

Create dedicated pages for top service areas. Each should include:
- City name in H1, title tag, meta description, and body content
- Reference to local landmarks, neighborhoods, or conditions (e.g., "The older homes in Huntington's Southside neighborhood often need...")
- Google Map embed showing service to that area
- "Serving [City] and surrounding areas" language

Priority city pages:
1. Huntington, WV (primary -- may be homepage)
2. Barboursville, WV
3. Hurricane, WV
4. Ashland, KY
5. Ironton, OH

### Phase 3: Blog Content (Ongoing Monthly)

Publish 2-4 blog posts per month targeting informational and long-tail keywords.

**Month 1 Blog Posts:**
- "How Much Does It Cost to Paint a House in Huntington, WV? (2026 Guide)"
- "Interior vs. Exterior Paint: What Huntington Homeowners Need to Know"

**Month 2 Blog Posts:**
- "The Best Exterior Paint Colors for Homes in the Tri-State Area"
- "5 Signs Your Huntington Home Needs Repainting"

**Month 3 Blog Posts:**
- "How to Choose a Painting Contractor in West Virginia"
- "Cabinet Painting vs. Replacement: What's Worth It?"

**Month 4 Blog Posts:**
- "Preparing Your Huntington Home for Exterior Painting Season"
- "Why Deck Staining Matters in West Virginia's Humid Climate"

**Ongoing Topics:**
- Seasonal painting guides (spring prep, fall touch-ups)
- Project spotlights / case studies with before/after photos
- Paint color trend posts
- "Cost of [specific service] in [city]" posts
- Neighborhood-specific content

### Phase 4: Review & Authority Building (Ongoing)

- **Google Reviews:** Systematically ask every completed job for a Google review. Aim for 25+ reviews in first 6 months.
- **Directory Listings:** Claim and optimize profiles on Angi, HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack, Houzz, Yelp, BBB, Nextdoor, Facebook.
- **Local Citations:** Ensure NAP consistency across 30+ directories.
- **Schema Markup:** LocalBusiness, Service, Review, FAQ schema on all relevant pages.
- **Google Business Profile:** Post weekly updates, respond to all reviews, add photos regularly.

## Technical SEO Checklist

| Element | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| **Site Speed** | Target < 2s load time. No competitor achieves this. |
| **Mobile-First** | 60%+ of local searches are mobile. Responsive design mandatory. |
| **Schema Markup** | LocalBusiness, Service, FAQ, Review, BreadcrumbList |
| **Title Tags** | Format: "[Service] in [City], WV | Meade Warner Painting" |
| **Meta Descriptions** | Include phone number, "free estimate," and city name |
| **H1 Tags** | One per page, include primary keyword |
| **Image Alt Text** | Describe service + location (e.g., "exterior house painting in Huntington WV") |
| **Internal Linking** | Link service pages to related blog posts and city pages |
| **XML Sitemap** | Submit to Google Search Console |
| **Google Search Console** | Set up day one. Monitor impressions and clicks. |
| **Google Business Profile** | Primary category: "Painter." Secondary: "Painting contractor." |
| **NAP Consistency** | Same name, address, phone across every listing |
| **SSL/HTTPS** | Required (standard with modern hosting) |
| **Core Web Vitals** | LCP < 2.5s, FID < 100ms, CLS < 0.1 |
